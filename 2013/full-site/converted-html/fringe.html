Friday, 27 September 2013

Not just a conference

Shropgeek (R)evolution is more than just a conference. We'll be paying host to a series of special events put on especially for our attendees, to give a flavour of the things that Shropgeek gets up to all year round.

Shropgeek Rebellion

6:30pm, Thursday 26 September 2013

Rebellion is our quarterly event featuring talks from industry folk from Shropshire. We're putting on a special Rebellion the night before the conference as a warm up. Featuring Shropshire residents Neil Kinnish and Jan Minihane. Find out more on Lanyrd.
