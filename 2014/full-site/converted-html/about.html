About

Shropshire's original web conference

This September, 200 people from just about every sort of digital job will join us in the 'Shire for a world-class, full-day web conference. Entrepreneurs, professionals and geeks of every description will share what makes things faster, stronger, and better in the town of Darwin's birth. This is the (R)Evolution's 5th year, and we're proud to say:

It keeps getting better!

Our speakers represent many digital disciplines â€“ from design and user experience, to strategy and production. Each one brings years of experience and will share their unique perspective.

We have always been a friendly conference, because we started (and continue!) as a gathering of like-minded digital professionals over beer and discussion. There's a lot to fit into a single day, but we always make room for discussion and questions.

We believe in supporting the UK's digital community, and welcome anyone interested in the web.
