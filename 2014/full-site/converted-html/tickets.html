Terms of sale

Tickets will be on general sale through Eventbrite from 10am on April 25th 2014.

A limited amount of earlybird tickets will be released first at the price of £69. Once these are sold out, standard tickets will go on sale at £89. Micro sponsor tickets will be available until all tickets are sold.

  • Shropgeek is not VAT registered and therefore VAT is not applied to the ticket price.
  • Eventbrite fees will be added to the cost of the ticket at checkout.
  • Paypal fees will not be passed on to you.

Micro Sponsors

Micro sponsorship is intended to give individuals and very small companies the opportunity to show their support. Think of it as your chance to support and encourage a "serious" cake habit. The more micro sponsors we get, the more cake we can provide in the breaks!

Student Tickets

Student tickets have been made available at the reduced price of £45. If you purchase a student ticket, you must produce valid student ID at registration when asked, otherwise you will be charged the full price of a standard ticket.

Student tickets do not include a t-shirt on the day or entry to the after party.
