Welcome back

After 3 years as an evening event, 2013 saw (R)Evolution evolve into a full day, single-track web conference. It was so awesome we are doing it all again!

Once again, we have 8 of the web's most revolutionary thinkers and space for 200 attendees. So whether you're a web industry veteran or just starting your career, you won't want to miss out on the chance to be part of one of the friendliest web conferences in the UK.

What's included

8 awesome speakers

Our world-class speakers represent many digital disciplines – from design and user experience, to strategy and production. Each one brings years of experience and will share their unique perspective.

200 attendees

You will meet people with just about every sort of digital job title. Entrepreneurs and businesspeople from all over the UK will be here. Name tags may include: designers, strategists, developers, engineers, analysts...

Lunch and plenty of free hot drinks

Tickets include lunch and plenty of coffee, with time and space to meet and chat with speakers, sponsors and attendees. No one will go hungry or under-caffeinated here.

An unforgettable after party

The (R)Evolution doesn't stop with the final talk. All are invited to join us at Romolo, with money behind the bar. There will be cocktails so varied, you may find design inspiration in the colours alone!

Supporting fringe events

A full-day event isn't big enough for everything we've got for the digital community. Before the main event, we put on a Rebellion at the Alb. After, there's an incredible cocktail party, breakfast the next morning and maybe even more to be announced.

All in the heart of stunning Shropshire

We call the 'Shire home, and welcome your visit. Shrewsbury offers culture, history and great food. Or, unplug before or after Shropshire's original web conference with a walk in the natural beauty of Shropshire's hills.

Who is it for

Designers

Whether it's layout and styling, colour palettes and typography or designing the users' journey and experience, we've got something to interest you.

Developers

Live and breathe code, or dabble and want to expand your skillset? We wouldn't consider this to be a "proper" Shropgeek conference without you!

Makers

Are you a maker, a builder of web things? Content and communities, apps or strategies, you're a creative type and a maker – we've got you covered.

With support from

Shropgeek

We are a collection of like minded people based in and around Shropshire; who like to meet regularly to catchup on the latest goings on and socialise with fellow geeks.
