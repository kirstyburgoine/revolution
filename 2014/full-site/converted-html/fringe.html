Shropgeek Rebellion

6:30pm, Thursday 25 September 2014

Rebellion is our quarterly event featuring talks from industry folk. We're putting on a special Rebellion the night before the conference, as a warm up. More details will be announced soon.

After Party

7:30pm, Friday 26 September 2014

You know what they say, if it ain't broke... Last year's after party has become something of a legend, so of course we are going to do it all over again! Held at Romolo, Shrewsbury's only waterside cocktail lounge, there will be some money behind the bar, some crazy cocktails and Shropshire's MisterFox as DJ!

Romolo, Shrewsbury, SY1 1HH

Shropgeek breakfast

9:30am, Saturday 27 September 2014

The morning after the conference, we'll be recovering in the Shrewsbury Coffeehouse, opposite the train station in the town centre. Come and enjoy a good chat over a high-quality cup of coffee and a bite to eat while you wait for your train home.

Shrewsbury Coffee House, Shrewsbury, SY1 2AE
