A Day the Earth Will Never Forget

After 3 years as an evening event, 2013 saw (R)Evolution evolve into a full day, single-track web conference. It was so spectacular we are doing it all for a third and final time!

We have 9 of the web's most revolutionary thinkers and space for 250 attendees. So whether you're a web industry veteran or just starting your career, you won't want to miss out on the chance to be part of one of the friendliest web conferences in the UK.

Who is it for?

Designers

Whether it's layout and styling, colour palettes and typography or designing the users' journey and experience, we've got something to interest you.

Developers

Live and breathe code, or dabble and want to expand your skillset? We wouldn't consider this to be a "proper" Shropgeek conference without you!

Makers

Are you a maker, a builder of web things? Content and communities, apps or strategies, you're a creative type and a maker – we've got you covered.
